Our Mission

Rise Engineering & Coding was founded with the mission to provide free STEM education to middle school students across Atlanta. We believe that early exposure to engineering and coding concepts can inspire the next generation of innovators and problem-solvers.

Our Story

As students passionate about STEM, we recognized the need for accessible, engaging STEM education in our community. We created Rise to share our knowledge and inspire younger students to explore the exciting worlds of engineering and coding.
