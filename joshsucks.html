RISE Turtle Robot Kit

Our signature hands-on STEM kit, given out free at RISE workshops for middle schoolers.

Designed by students, for students—no experience needed. Build, code, and create your own drawing robot!

How does it work?

  1. Come to a RISE workshop. We host free hands-on events for Atlanta-area middle schoolers (see our homepage for upcoming dates).
  2. Get your kit for free! Every attendee receives a Turtle Robot Kit packed with everything you need. No sign-up fees, no catch.
  3. Build and code together. Our student mentors help you assemble your robot and walk you through the basics of robotics and coding, step by step.
  4. Take it home. Your robot is yours to keep—keep creating, experimenting, and showing off your skills!
Teachers: Want Turtle Kits for your classroom? Email us—we’ll do our best to help!
