Get to Know RISE Kits

Discover how our hands-on STEM kits help students connect digital learning with real-world building, coding, and engineering—all with easy-to-follow guides and supportive mentorship.

  1. What is a RISE Kit?
    Each RISE Kit comes with 3D-printed parts, electronics, and clear instructions to spark curiosity and teach engineering and coding skills through creative, project-based activities.
  2. What makes them special?
    • Hands-on building and assembly
    • Programming and coding integration
    • Real-world engineering concepts
    • Step-by-step learning materials
  3. How can you get a RISE Kit?
    1. Attend a Workshop: Join our free hands-on events, where you'll build and code your own kit with help from student mentors. Every participant gets to take home their kit. See upcoming workshops.
    2. Through Your School: We partner with teachers and educational organizations to bring RISE Kits to classrooms, clubs, and enrichment programs. Contact us if you'd like to introduce RISE Kits at your school.
