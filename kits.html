RISE Kits

Our signature hands-on STEM kits, given out free at RISE workshops for middle schoolers.

Designed by students, for students—no experience needed. Empowering tomorrow's engineers, one kit at a time.

How does it work?

  1. Come to a RISE workshop. We host free hands-on events for Atlanta-area middle schoolers (see our homepage for upcoming dates).
  2. Get your kit for free! Every attendee receives a RISE kit packed with everything you need. No sign-up fees, no catch.
  3. Build and code together. Our student mentors guide you through assembling your kit and teach you the skills you need, one step at a time.
  4. Take it home. Your kit is yours to keep—keep creating, experimenting, and showing off your skills!
Teachers: Want RISE Kits for your classroom? Email us—we’ll do our best to help!
