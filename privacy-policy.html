Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2025

At RISE STEM, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect any personal information you provide to us through our website.

1. Information We Collect

When you submit a message through our contact form, we collect:

  • Your email address
  • Any message or information you include in the form

2. How We Use Your Information

We only use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your inquiries
  • Provide support related to your message

We do not use your data for marketing, advertising, or analytics purposes.

3. How Your Information Is Handled

Our contact form is powered by Formspree, a third-party service that securely processes and forwards your message to our email inbox. You can review their privacy policy here: https://formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

We do not sell or share your personal information with any other third parties.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect the information you provide from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

If you would like us to delete your submitted message or personal information, please contact us at contact@risestem.org.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please email us at contact@risestem.org.
